What GeoRetire Does

How GeoRetire Works

1

Input Your Profile

Enter your age, retirement target, savings, contributions, risk tolerance, and zip codes for current and planned retirement location.

2

COL Adjustment

Your retirement zip code is mapped to a cost-of-living index to compute your true location-adjusted income requirement — not a national average.

3

Portfolio Projection

We apply time value of money, compound growth, and present value formulas to project your portfolio trajectory from now through retirement.

4

Monte Carlo Simulation

10,000 market-outcome scenarios are simulated using historical return distributions to produce a realistic probability of success — not a single-path estimate.

5

Behavioral Insights

Your results are analyzed through a behavioral finance lens to surface biases, risks, and ranked action items that maximize your probability of success.
